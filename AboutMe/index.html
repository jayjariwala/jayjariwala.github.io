About Me

General Questions


Q: Tell me about yourself.
A: I am very passionate about coding and technology. I mostly spend my time in library working on special interest projects. I am currently focusing on finishing Front End and Back End certification of FreeCodeCamp.

Q: Tell me about your Education.
A: Since my childhood, I always had a sense of wonder about Science and Technology so I decided to pursue Engineering in Information Technology. I completed my under-graduation in 2014 from Gujarat Technological University, India. Currently I am pursuing master degree in Computer Science at Fairleigh Dickinson University, NJ.

Q: How did you get into coding?
A: I fall in love with coding, when I created my first Dynamic web application called Alumni Network with my teammates during the final year of my college. I felt great satisfaction because we were able accomplish what we imagined. From that time onwards I have always been dedicating most of my time learning to code as much as I can.

Q: What is your ideal company?
A: An ideal company would be the one which helps me to evolve my technological knowledge according to the latest technological trends.

Q: If you had to choose one, would you consider yourself a big-picture person or detail-oriented person?
A: I would say I am more of a detail-oriented person, but both sides have a equal importance.

Q: What are your weaknesses ?
A: I have glossophobia (Fear of public speaking) , but I think I am overcoming this fear though getting more socialized with my professors and classmates.
